## Reel/TikTok Ideas

### Quick Hits (15-30 seconds)

1. **"The markup trap"**
   - "You're charging 35% markup thinking you're making 35% profit..."
   - Show the math on screen
   - "You're actually making 26%."
   - Hook: "Here's the fix"

2. **"Your first marketing budget"**
   - "How much should you spend on marketing when you're starting out?"
   - Pause
   - "Zero. You need 10 people who trust you. That's it."

3. **"The uncomfortable price"**
   - "If quoting a price feels slightly uncomfortable..."
   - Pause
   - "You're probably in the right range."

4. **"Cash flow kills"**
   - "The #1 reason contractors fail isn't lack of work."
   - "It's cash flow."
   - "You can be booked solid and still go broke."

5. **"Looking professional day one"**
   - Speed run: business cards, logo (Canva), Google profile, quote template
   - "Professional in 30 minutes. No excuses."

### Longer Form (60 seconds)

1. **"What I wish I knew before starting"**
   - Format: talking head with text overlays
   - Hit 3-4 key lessons from the startup guide

2. **"The real startup costs"**
   - Show realistic ranges by trade
   - "Painter: $500-2,000. Plumber: $25,000-40,000."
   - "Know your number before you start."

3. **"How to price your first job"**
   - Walk through the formula
   - "Materials + Labor + Overhead + Profit = Price"
   - "Don't skip that last one."

- Total: $1,800

They think they're making $300 profit.

Here's what they forgot:

- Insurance
- Phone
