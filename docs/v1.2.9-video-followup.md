## Draft (two paragraphs)

> Heads up — v1.2.9 just shipped and it lights up card payment acceptance for everyone on Pro and Premium. > Most of you are going to keep using Venmo and Zelle for the majority of your jobs because that's what your customers already use and there's nothing to set up. That's fine — that's the point. Cards are there for the customer who shows up with a credit card and no Venmo. Setup is in the app: Business Settings → Card Payments. Stripe handles ID and bank verification on their side; we just show your customer the button once Stripe says you're verified. If you've got questions about how Stripe works (payouts, refunds, disputes), the answer is almost always "Stripe Dashboard." We're the rails; Stripe is the processor.
