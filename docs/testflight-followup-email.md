## Pro Tips for Follow-Ups

### Timing
- First follow-up: 3 days after invite sent
- Second follow-up: 7 days after install
- Final follow-up: 14 days (if no response)

### Tone
- Keep it casual and friendly
- Don't guilt trip inactive testers
- Make it easy to respond (ask specific questions)
- Show appreciation constantly

### What Works
- Short emails get more responses
- Specific questions beat "any feedback?"
- Share other tester insights (anonymously)
- Show you're acting on feedback

### What Doesn't Work
- Long surveys (keep it conversational)
- Generic "how's it going?" (too vague)
- Pressure or urgency (they're doing you a favor)
- Radio silence (check in regularly)

---

**Remember:** Testers are volunteers. Be grateful, responsive, and respectful of their time!
