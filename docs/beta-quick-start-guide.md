## Step-by-Step: Creating Your First Quote

1. **Start New Quote:** Tap the **+** button in the upper right on the Dashboard or Quotes screen.

2. **Enter Details:** Fill in the **project name** and **client name** (both required). Optionally add a description and quote reference number.

3. **Add Materials:**
   - Tap "Add Materials" to browse the product catalog
   - Search or browse by category (Framing, Drywall, Electrical, Plumbing, etc.)
   - Tap the **+** button next to items to add them to your quote
   - Use the stepper controls to adjust quantities
   - Tap "Add Items" to continue, or "Done" when finished

4. **Add Labor Costs:**
   - Enter your labor cost in the "Labor" field
   - This is added separately from materials

5. **Materials (Quick Estimate):**
   - If you already know the total materials cost, enter it here
   - No need to add individual items from the database if using this method

6. **Set Markup:** Adjust the markup percentage to reflect your profit margin (default is typically 20-30%).

7. **Review & Export:**
   - Tap "Review" to see the complete quote summary
   - Tap "PDF" to generate a professional PDF with your company branding
   - Tap "CSV" to export a spreadsheet for your records
   - Tap "Done" to save as a draft for editing later

**IMPORTANT NOTES:**
- Add your company details in Settings ‚Üí Company Details for branded PDFs
- The markup percentage is automatically removed from customer-facing PDFs (they see final price only)
- All quotes are saved locally on your device and work offline

**Tip:** You can edit quote details anytime before sending. Just tap the quote from the list to open the editor.

## Managing Quotes

- **Viewing Quotes:** From the Quotes tab, see all your saved quotes in a list sorted by most recent.
- **Filtering & Searching:** Use the search bar to find quotes by client name, project name, or status. Filter by status using the filter button.
- **Swipe Gestures:**
  - Swipe **left** to duplicate a quote (creates a copy with new ID)
  - Swipe **right** to delete a quote (confirmation required)
  - **Tap the quote card** to open and edit
- **Status Changes:**
  - Tap a quote to open the editor
  - Tap the **status chip** at the top to change status
  - Available statuses: Draft ‚Üí Sent ‚Üí Approved ‚Üí Completed ‚Üí Archived
- **Pin Important Quotes:** Tap the pin icon on a quote card to keep it at the top of your list for quick access.

**Tip:** Use consistent status workflows (Draft ‚Üí Sent ‚Üí Approved ‚Üí Completed) to track your pipeline and see dashboard stats.

## Assemblies (Pro Feature)

**What are Assemblies?** Assemblies are reusable product bundles for common jobs (e.g., "Frame a Room" or "Wire an Outlet"). Create them once, use them in multiple quotes to save time.

- **Accessing Assemblies:**
  - Tap the menu icon (‚ò∞) ‚Üí "Pro Tools" or "Assemblies" tab
  - All beta testers have Pro access to test this feature

- **Using Pre-made Assemblies:**
  - Browse the library of built-in assemblies
  - Tap an assembly to view its products and total cost
  - Tap "Add to Quote" when creating/editing a quote to insert all items at once

- **Creating Custom Assemblies:**
  - Tap "Assembly Manager" from the Pro Tools menu
  - Tap the **+** button to create a new assembly
  - Name your assembly (e.g., "Bathroom Rough-In" or "Kitchen Cabinet Install")
  - Search and add products from the QuoteCat catalog
  - Adjust quantities for each product
  - Tap "Save" to add it to your library

- **Editing Assemblies:**
  - Assemblies now show a collapsible "Current Products" section
  - Use **+/‚àí** stepper controls to adjust quantities inline
  - Add more products by searching in the "Add More Products" section below

**Pro Tip:** Assemblies are perfect for jobs you do repeatedly! Create assemblies for your most common service packages to build quotes 10x faster.
