## Step 1: Open QuoteCat

Launch the app from your home screen. You'll see the **Dashboard** with your business stats.

**What You'll See:**
- Welcome header with business summary
- Quick Stats cards (All Quotes, Pinned, Draft, Sent, Approved, Completed)
- Value Tracking (Pending, Approved, To Invoice)
- Pinned Quotes section
- Recent Quotes section
- Beautiful gradient background (changes with dark mode!)

**Navigation:**
- **4 tabs** via drawer menu (top-left or swipe from left):
  - Dashboard (home screen)
  - Quotes (full quote list)
  - Pro Tools (assemblies and premium features)
  - Settings

**Important:** There is NO wizard or multi-step flow. Everything happens on a single editor screen.

---

## Step 2: Create Your First Quote

### Start a New Quote

1. **Tap the orange "+" button** (top-right corner)
2. You'll land on the **Quote Editor** screen

### Enter Quote Details

Fill in the basic info at the top:

- **Project Name:** "Kitchen Remodel" (required)
- **Client Name:** "John Smith" (required)
- **Status:** Leave as "Draft" for now (you can change it later)

### Add Materials

1. **Tap "Add materials" button** (under the Materials section)
2. **Browse categories** using the horizontal tabs:
   - Framing, Drywall, Electrical, Plumbing, Flooring, Roofing, Paint, Hardware
3. **Add products:**
   - Find a product (e.g., "2x4x8 Stud")
   - Use the **+/- buttons** to set quantity
   - A quantity badge appears on products you've selected
   - Try adding 3-5 different products
4. **Check your selection** at the top: "X items, $XXX"
5. **Tap "Add X items"** button at the bottom
6. You're back on the Quote Editor with your materials added!

**Important:** You must use the +/- stepper controls. Tapping a product doesn't add it automatically.

### Add Labor Cost

1. **Scroll down to "Labor" field**
2. **Enter total labor amount** as a dollar value (e.g., "$1,200")
3. The app will auto-format it to 2 decimals

**Important:** There is NO hours √ó rate calculation. Just enter the total labor cost.

### Add Markup (Optional)

1. **Scroll to "Markup Percentage" field**
2. Enter a percentage (e.g., "15")
3. The % symbol is added automatically
4. Watch the Grand Total update

### Review Totals

Check the **Quote Totals Card** at the bottom:
- Materials: (sum of all items)
- Labor: (what you entered)
- Subtotal
- Markup (if applied)
- **Grand Total** (in orange)

### Export Your Quote

1. **Tap "Review & Export"** button (top-right)
2. You'll see the **Review Screen** with:
   - Company details (if you added them in Settings)
   - Quote header (project, client, date)
   - All line items
   - Cost breakdown
3. **Export options:**
   - **"Export PDF"** ‚Üí Generate PDF, share via email/AirDrop/etc.
   - **"CSV"** ‚Üí Export to Excel/Sheets

**Done!** Your first quote is created. üéâ

## Step 3: Manage Your Quotes

### View Quote List

1. **Navigate to Quotes tab** (via drawer menu or back button)
2. You'll see all your quotes in a scrollable list

**Quote Cards Show:**
- Project name (bold)
- Client name
- Status chip (color-coded)
- Total amount (large, orange)
- Last updated date
- Star icon (for pinning)

### Filter Quotes

Use the **horizontal filter chips** at the top:
- All, Pinned, Draft, Sent, Approved, Completed, Archived
- Tap any chip to filter

### Search Quotes

Use the **search bar** to find quotes by:
- Project name
- Client name

### Swipe Gestures

- **Swipe LEFT** ‚Üí Duplicate quote
- **Swipe RIGHT** ‚Üí Delete quote (3-second undo appears)
- **Tap star icon** ‚Üí Pin/unpin quote

**Important:** Swipe gestures are reversed from the old guide!

### Change Quote Status

1. **Tap a quote** to open the editor
2. **Tap the status chip** at the top (Draft, Sent, Approved, Completed, Archived)
3. Select new status
4. Status updates automatically

**Status Workflow:**
```
Draft ‚Üí Sent ‚Üí Approved ‚Üí Completed ‚Üí Archived
```

### Pin Important Quotes

1. **Tap the star icon** on any quote card
2. Pinned quotes appear:
   - At the top of the quote list
   - In the "Pinned Quotes" section on Dashboard
   - In the Dashboard Quick Stats ("Pinned" card)

## Step 4: Explore the Dashboard

Go back to the **Dashboard** tab to see your business overview.

### Quick Stats (Tap to Filter)

Each card is **clickable** - tap to filter quotes:
- **All Quotes** ‚Üí See all quotes
- **Pinned** ‚Üí See only pinned quotes
- **Draft, Sent, Approved, Completed** ‚Üí Filter by status

### Value Tracking

See the dollar value of your quotes by status:
- **Pending:** Total value of Draft + Sent quotes
- **Approved:** Total value of approved quotes (work to do)
- **To Invoice:** Total value of completed quotes (ready to bill)

**Use this to track your sales pipeline!**

### Customize Dashboard

1. **Go to Settings** ‚Üí **Dashboard**
2. Toggle sections on/off:
   - Quick Stats
   - Value Tracking
   - Pinned Quotes
   - Recent Quotes
3. Set how many recent quotes to show (3, 5, 10, or all)
4. Tap "Reset to Default" to restore original layout

## Step 5: Try Assemblies (Pro Feature)

**What are Assemblies?**
Pre-built material templates that save time on common jobs. Example: "Frame a Wall" automatically adds studs, plates, screws, etc.

**Note:** Assemblies are a **Pro feature**. Free tier users will see an upgrade prompt.

### Access Assemblies

1. **Go to Pro Tools tab** (via drawer menu)
2. **Tap "Assembly Library"** or **"Assembly Manager"**

**Important:** Assemblies are NOT a separate bottom tab.

### Browse Built-in Assemblies

You'll see pre-built templates:
- Frame a Wall
- Drywall a Room
- Wire a Room
- Install Flooring
- And more...

### Use an Assembly in a Quote

1. **Create or open a quote**
2. **Tap "Add from Assembly"** button (below "Add materials")
3. **Select an assembly** from the library
4. **Enter variables** (e.g., room length, width, height)
5. **Preview calculated materials**
6. **Tap "Add to Quote"**
7. All items added instantly with correct quantities!

### Create a Custom Assembly (Pro)

1. **Go to Pro Tools** ‚Üí **Assembly Manager**
2. **Tap "+ New"** button
3. **Enter assembly name:** "Standard Bathroom Plumbing"
4. **Add products** from the catalog
5. **Set quantities** (fixed or formula-based)
6. **Save**
7. Use it in future quotes!

**Go to Settings tab** to configure the app.

### Add Company Details

1. **Tap "Company Details"** (under Quote Defaults)
2. **Fill in:**

## ‚ùì FAQ

**Q: Do I need to create an account?**
A: Not yet! All data is stored locally on your device. Cloud sync coming soon.

**Q: Will my data be lost when the app updates?**
A: No. TestFlight updates preserve your data. We use forward-compatible data models.

**Q: Can I test on multiple devices?**
A: Yes! Install TestFlight on each device and use your invite code. But data doesn't sync between devices yet (local-only for now).

**Q: Is this the final app design?**
A: Very close! We're polishing based on your feedback, but core features are complete.

**Q: When does the beta end?**
A: We'll run beta for 2-4 weeks, then launch to the App Store.

**Q: Can I use this for real quotes?**
A: Absolutely! The app is production-ready. But note: product prices are estimates, so verify with your supplier.

**Q: What's the difference between Free and Pro?**
- **Free:** 25 quotes total, 5 PDFs/month, 2 CSVs/month, no assemblies
- **Pro:** Unlimited quotes/exports, custom assemblies, cloud backup (coming soon)

**Q: What happens to my quotes if I don't upgrade to Pro?**
A: You keep all your quotes! Free tier is fully functional. Pro just adds advanced features.

**Q: Can I invite friends to test?**
A: Not yet - we're starting with a small group. We may open more slots later!

**Q: Why don't some materials have accurate prices?**
A: Right now we're using seed data (sample prices). In Phase 1, we'll integrate with 1Build API for real-time pricing from Lowe's, Home Depot, and Menards.
