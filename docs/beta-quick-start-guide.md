## Getting Started

### Launching QuoteCat

1. Install QuoteCat Beta from your TestFlight invitation email or app.
2. Open QuoteCat by tapping the app icon on your device.

### Dashboard Overview

- **At a Glance:** View recent quotes (once you have them), quick stats, and navigation shortcuts right from the Dashboard.
- **Navigation Menu:** Use the menu icon in the upper left to switch between Dashboard, Quotes, **Pro Tools**, and Settings.

### Customizing Settings

- **Company Details:** Enter your business name, logo (coming soon), and contact info for branded quotes.
- **Dark Mode:** Toggle dark/light mode for comfortable viewing.
- **Usage Limits:** Check your quote and assembly usage (limits apply for free tiers).
- **Privacy Controls:** Manage data sharing and backup preferences.

**Note:** Settings changes save automatically.

---

## Step-by-Step: Creating Your First Quote

1. **Start New Quote:** Tap the **+** button in the upper right on the Dashboard.

2. **Enter Details:** Fill in the **project name and client name** (both required).

3. **Add Materials:**
   - Tap "Add Materials"
   - Select items from the saved list (database data)
   - Select "Add items" to keep working, or select "Done" when ready.

4. **Add Labor:**
   - Enter your cost

5. **Materials (Quick Estimate):** This is here if you know the cost already. No need to add materials from the database.

6. **Set Markup:** Adjust the markup percentage to reflect your profit margin.

7. **Review & Export:** Check the summary, then tap "PDF" or "CSV" to export. Or tap "Done" to save as a draft.

8. **Export to PDF or CSV:** Tap PDF to generate a PDF or share via text, email, or other apps. CSV works the same.

**NOTE:** Add custom business details in the app settings, under the menu icon (top left). Additionally, markup percentage is removed from the PDF for your customer.

**Tip:** You can edit quote details anytime before sending.

## Managing Quotes

- **Viewing Quotes:** From the Quotes tab, see all your saved quotes in a list.
- **Filtering & Searching:** Use the search bar or filters to find quotes by client, project, or status.
- **Swipe Gestures:** Swipe **left to duplicate**, swipe **right to delete**. **Tap the quote card** to edit.
- **Status Changes:** Tap a quote to open the editor, then tap the **status chip** at the top to change status (e.g., Draft, Sent, Approved, Completed, Archived).
- **Pin Important Quotes:** Star or pin key quotes for easy access at the top of your list.

**Tip:** Keep your quote list organized with tags or custom labels.

---

## Exploring the Dashboard

- **Quick Stats:** Instantly see total value of quotes and status breakdowns.
- **Quote Value:** Monitor your quoting volume at a glance.
- **Widget Customization:** Can be changed under Menu > Settings > Dashboard

**Note:** Data visualizations may change as we add new features—please share your feedback!

## Assemblies (Pro Feature)

- **Accessing Assemblies:** Tap "Pro Tools" under the app menu (Pro users only).
- **Using Assemblies:** Browse the library of pre-made assemblies or select one to add to a quote.
- **Creating Custom Assemblies:**
  - Tap "Assembly Manager."
  - Name your assembly, then add materials from the QuoteCat database.
  - Save and use in future quotes to speed up the process.

**Pro Tip:** Assemblies are perfect for recurring jobs or complex projects!
