## Tips for Submitting:

### Before You Apply:

1. **Register your business entity** (LLC or Corporation) if you haven't already
2. **Set up your business website** (quotecat.ai) with a professional About page
3. **Get an EIN** (Employer Identification Number) from the IRS if possible
4. **Create a professional email** (e.g., yourname@quotecat.ai instead of gmail)

### During Application:

1. **Be professional and concise** - Don't oversell, just state facts
2. **Emphasize mutual benefit** - You're driving sales to them
3. **Show you're legitimate** - Real business, real customers, real use case
4. **Be transparent about volume** - Low volume is actually good (less risk for them)
5. **Commit to attribution** - Makes them look good to have their name in contractor quotes

### After Submission:

1. **Follow up in 5-7 business days** if no response
2. **Be patient** - API approvals can take 2-4 weeks
3. **Be ready to sign agreements** - They may require commercial terms
4. **Ask about pricing** - Some APIs are free for low volume, others have fees

### If Denied or No Response:

1. **Don't be discouraged** - Move to Plan B (third-party services)
2. **Ask for feedback** - "Is there anything we could do to qualify?"
3. **Consider applying again** in 6 months with more traction (active users, testimonials)
