Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about QuoteCat

  • How is this different from spreadsheets?

    QuoteCat is mobile-first. Create quotes on your phone in 5 minutes while you're still on the job site. No laptop required, no complicated formulas. Just pick products, add labor, and send a professional PDF instantly.

  • Can I try it before paying?

    Absolutely! The free tier gives you 25 quotes per month and 5 PDF exports. No credit card required. Test it on real jobs, and upgrade only when you're ready for unlimited quotes and cloud backup.

  • What about my existing quotes?

    Your quotes are stored locally on your device (free tier) or backed up to the cloud (Pro/Premium). When you upgrade to Pro, we'll automatically migrate all your local quotes to the cloud so you never lose anything.

  • Does this work offline?

    Yes! QuoteCat works completely offline. Create quotes, calculate costs, and generate PDFs even without internet. Cloud sync happens automatically when you're back online (Pro tier only).

  • Can I use my own pricing?

    100%. Our catalog includes suggested retail prices, but you can override any price. Set your own markup percentages, add overhead, and adjust labor rates. Your pricing, your way.

  • What trades does this support?

    We have 100+ products across all major trades: framing, drywall, electrical, plumbing, HVAC, painting, flooring, concrete, roofing, and landscaping. More products added regularly based on user feedback.

  • Can I add my company logo?

    Yes! Pro tier includes your company name, email, phone, website, and address on every PDF. Premium tier adds your custom logo for maximum brand recognition.

  • What's the Quote Wizard?

    The Quote Wizard (Premium tier) is a guided assistant that asks simple questions like "How many square feet?" and "How many outlets?" then automatically builds the entire quote with accurate material quantities. Perfect for standardized jobs.

  • Do you offer refunds?

    Yes. If you're not satisfied within the first 30 days, email us and we'll refund you immediately, no questions asked. We're confident you'll love QuoteCat, but we want you to feel safe trying it.

  • How do I cancel my subscription?

    You can cancel anytime from your account settings. Your Pro/Premium features will remain active until the end of your billing period. When you cancel, your quotes stay safe in the cloud (read-only) so you never lose your data.

  • Is my data secure?

    Absolutely. All data is encrypted in transit and at rest. We use industry-standard security practices and never sell your data. Your quotes, clients, and pricing are yours alone. Read our Privacy Policy for details.

  • Can I use this on multiple devices?

    Pro and Premium tiers include multi-device sync. Use QuoteCat on your iPhone, iPad, and even switch devices mid-project. Everything stays in sync automatically via the cloud.
