Why Billable Hours Matter

You won't bill 40 hours a week. Between estimates, travel, admin, and callbacks, most contractors bill 25-35 hours per week. Be realistic or you'll undercharge.

Pro tip: If your rate seems too high, reduce overhead or increase billable hours before lowering your price.
